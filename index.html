Mision

Our vision is to become the best option for caregivers and family members all around the world. Reducing the number of caregivers who suffer from anxiety- “it is estimated that between half and three-quarters of caregivers suffer anxiety and depression"⁵.

Vision

Our mission is to help Alzheimer's Caregivers through all their processes of taking care of the patient; from the beginning when the disease was diagnosed until after the patient has passed away. “Trying to help people who take care of others”- AEME

Company description

 AEME is a social enterprise located in Mexico that looks out for the Good Health and Well Being(SDG 3) and Decent Work and Economic Work (SD 8) of Alzheimer's' Caregivers by using an app. This app named No me olvides provides a support network among caregivers and people who live through similar situations. It will allow them, to subsequent a process of interviewing and validating with our associates, to be able to acquire an official certification after gaining experiences and knowledge of caregiving their patients.

Product and Service description

No me olvides is a free app easy to use. Its’ main goal is to support and help Alzheimer’s caregivers by providing true information about the disease provided by specialists; associations and places that can be useful in case of need; and a support network where caregivers can meet people who live through the same situation sharing their experiences. Many apps provide information, but none of them come with a support network; making No me olvides different from other apps. The app was developed with the Thunkable platform using Firebase for storing the user data and Procreate for some of the illustrations.
